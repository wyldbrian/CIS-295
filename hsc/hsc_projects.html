This page covers some of the projects we've been working on as a group. Feel free to scroll through and check them all out!

Mobile Cart Build

01/30/2018

For this session we built out our mobile cart for moving our lab between locations. Although this night wasn't filled with technology demos and information, we got a chance to discuss the roadmap for our club. Thank you to everyone who helped setup our lab environment! We look forward to the next session!

RetroPie Build

01/23/2018

This week we had fun with Pi! We spent some time setting up a Raspberry Pi Model 3 B with RetroPie, and testing some of it's capabilities. Additionally, we had a chance to cover some Raspberry Pi use cases, including web, chat, nas, etc. Thank you to everyone who attended!

Initial Server Buildout

12/14/2017

This week was our first session! A few different people brought in hardware, and we got a chance to discuss how easy it ease to build your own server at home. We covered using various tools online to choose components and put together inexpensive builds. Additionally, we discussed projects we're interested in working on, and started organizing them into upcoming sessions. Thank you to everyone who joined us for our first session!

If you'd like to see a particular technology, please visit our contact page and send us a message!
