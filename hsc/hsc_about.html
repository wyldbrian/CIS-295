The Home Server Club is a group of people who meet regularly to discuss and share project ideas related to building out and configuring physical machines, applications, network devices, and more. The group typically meets on Monday evenings after work to go over various ideas, demo projects, and help each other overcome roadblocks.

The group was formed in November 2017 and has 50+ members.

If you're interested in joining the group, please see our contact section for details on how to reach us.
