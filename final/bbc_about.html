Bandon Baking Company has been owned and operated by Chris and Kim for the last 20 years! After a fishing trip went south and left Chris and his father crabbing from the docks, they decided to visit the previous owner. They called about an ad, bought some pastries, and the rest is history.

Since purchasing the bakery, the menu has expanded to include breakfast and lunch. Additionally, Bandon Baking Company now offers catering and events.
